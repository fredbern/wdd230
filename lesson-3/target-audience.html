Target audience

Descripton

The website is targeting the tourists and local of any age who wants to explore the four islands of FSM.

Two Perosnas

Berny is a tourist who wants to explore the seven water falls on Pohnpei island. He knows that it is very risky to go there when it is raining. He pull up the website and check the weather.

John is a local who wants to take his family to the outer island of Pohnpei on a picnic. He wants to makes sure there is no rain on that day. He open his cellphone and check it on this website.
