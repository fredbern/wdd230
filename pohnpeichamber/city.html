Pohnpei

Historical demographic

The population of the state in 2010 was approximately 36,196. While the majority of the population consider themselves ethnic Pohnpeians, Pohnpei is more ethnically diverse than any other island in the FSM.

Current demographic

The population of the state in 2021 was approximately 36,896. The population increases by 1.8 percent.
